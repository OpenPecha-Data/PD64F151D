10

śakro jinasya paripṛcchati devarājocaramāṇa prajña katha yujyati bodhisattvo|aṇumātra yo na
khalu yujyati skandhadhātauyo eva yujyati (sa yujyati) bodhisattvaḥ||1||

cirayānaprasthitu sa vedayitavya sattvobahubuddhakoṭinayutehi kṛtādhikāro|yo śrutva dharmi imi
nirmitamāyakalpāṃna ca kāṅkṣate ayu prayujyati śikṣamāṇaḥ||2||

kāntāramārgi puruṣo bahu(bhī)janehigopāla sīma vanasaṃpada paśyate yo|āśvāsaprāpta bhavatī na
ca tasya trāsoabhyāśa grāmanagarāṇa ime nimittāḥ||3||

emeva prajñavarapāramitā jinānāṃśṛṇu tāta yo labhati bodhi gaveṣamāṇaḥ|āśvāsaprāpta bhavatī
na ca tasya trāsonārhantabhūmi na pi pratyayabuddhabhūmī||4||

puruṣo hi sāgarajalaṃ vraji paśyanāyasaci paśyate drumavanaspatiśailarājam|athavā na paśyati
nimitta nikāṅkṣa bhotia(bhyāśa)to mahasamudra na so'tidūre||5||

emeva bodhivaraprasthitu veditavyośruṇamāṇa prajña imi pāramitā jinānām|yadyāpi saṃmukha na
vyākṛtu nāyakenotathapi spṛśiṣyati nacireṇa hu buddhabodhim||6||

suvasanti kāli patite tṛṇapatraśākhānacireṇa patraphalapuṣpa samāgamanti|prajñāya pāramita yasyimu
hastaprāptānacireṇa bodhivara prāpsyati nāyakānām||7||

yatha istri gurviṇi ya ceṣṭati vedanābhijñātavyu kālu ayamasya prajāyanāya|tatha bodhisattva
śruṇamāṇu jināna prajñāṃrati chanda vīkṣati spṛśiṣyati bodhi śīghram||8||

caramāṇu prajñavarapāramitāya yogīna ca rūpavṛddhi na ca paśyati pārihāṇim|dharmā adharma imu
paśyati dharmadhātuṃna ca nirvṛtiṃ spṛśati so viharāti prajñām||9||

caramāṇu yo na iha kalpayi buddhadharmāṃbala ṛddhipāda na ca kalpayi bodhi śāntām|avikalpakalpavigato
adhiṣṭhānacārīeṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā||10||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ
dhāraṇīguṇaparivarto nāma daśamaḥ||
