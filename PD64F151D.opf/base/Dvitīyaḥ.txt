rupasmi yo na sthihate na ca vedanāyāṃsaṃjñāya yo na sthihate na ca cetanāyām|vijñāni yo na
sthihate sthitu dharmatāyāṃeṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā||1||

nityamanityasukhaduḥkhaśubhāśubhaṃ tiātmanyanātmi tathatā ta(tha) śūnyatāyām|phalaprāptitāya
athito arahantabhūmaupratyekabhūmiathito tatha buddhabhūmau||2||

yatha nāyako'sthitaku dhātuasaṃskṛtāyātatha saṃskṛtāya athito aniketacārī|evaṃ ca sthānu athito
sthita bodhisattvoasthānu sthānu ayu sthānu jinena ukto||3||

yo icchatī sugataśrāvaka haṃ bhaveyaṃpratyekabuddha bhaviyāṃ tatha dharmarājo|imu kṣāntyanāgami
na śakyati prāpuṇetuṃyatha ārapāragamanāya atītadarśī||4||

yo dharma bhāṣyati ya bhāṣyati bhāṣyamāṇāṃphalaprāpta pratyayajino tatha lokanātho|nirvāṇato
adhigato vidupaṇḍitehisarve ta ātmaja nidṛṣṭa tathāgatena||5||

catvāri pudgala ime na trasanti ye'sminjinaputra satyakuśalo avivartiyaśca|arhaṃ vidhūtamalakleśa
prahīṇakāṅkṣokalyāṇamitraparipācita yaścaturthaḥ||6||

evaṃ carantu vidu paṇḍitu bodhisattvonārhaṃmi śikṣati na pratyayabuddhabhūmau|sarvajñatāya anuśikṣati
buddhadharmeśikṣāaśikṣa na ya śikṣati eṣa śikṣā||7||

na ca rupavṛddhiparihāṇiparigrahāyena ca śikṣati vividhadharmaparigrahāye|sarvajñatāṃ ca parigṛhṇati
śikṣamāṇoniryāyatī ya iya śikṣa guṇe ratānām||8||

rupe na prajña iti rupi na asti prajñāvijñāna saṃjña api vedana cetanā ca|na ca eti prajña iti
teṣa na asti prajñāākāśadhātusama tasya na cāsti bhedaḥ||9||

ārambaṇāna prakṛtī sa a(na)ntapārāsattvāna yā ca prakṛtī sa anantapārā|ākāśadhātuprakṛtī sa
anantapārāprajñā pi lokavidunāṃ sa anantapārā||10||

saṃjñeti nāma parikīrtitu nāyakenasaṃjñāṃ vibhāviya prahāṇa vrajanti pāram|ye atra saṃjñavigamaṃ
anuprāpnuvantite pāraprāpta sthita pāramite hu bhonti||11||

saci gaṅgavālukasamāni sthihitva kalpāṃsattveti śabda parikīrtayi nāyako'yam|sattvasyupādu kutu
bheṣyati ādiśuddhoeṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā||12||

evaṃ jino bhaṇati apratikūlabhāṇīyadahaṃ imāya varapāramitāya āsī|tada vyākṛto ahu parāpuruṣottamenabuddho
bhaviṣyasi anāgataadhvanasmin||13||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ śakraparivarto
nāma dvitīyaḥ||
