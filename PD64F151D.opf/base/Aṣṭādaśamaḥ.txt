18

gambhīra rupa api vedana cetanā cavijñāna saṃjña prakṛtī animittaśāntā|kāṇḍena gādha yatha sāgari
eṣamāṇoprajñāya skandha vimṛṣitva alabdhagādhā||1||

yo bodhisattva imu budhyati eva dharmāṃgambhīrayānaparamārthanirupalepān|yasmin na skandha na
pi āyatanaṃ na dhātukiṃ vā svapuṇyasamudāgamu kiṃci tasya||2||

yatha rāgadharmacaritaḥ puruṣaḥ striyāyesaṃketa kṛtva alabhantu vivartayeyā|yāvanti cittacaritā
divasena tasyatāvanta kalpa anubudhyati bodhisattvo||3||

yo bodhisattva bahukalpasahasrakoṭayodānaṃ dadeyu vimalaṃ tatha śīla rakṣe|yaścaiva prajñavarapāramitāprayuktodharmaṃ
bhaṇeya kala puṇya na dānaśīle||4||

yo bodhisattva varaprajña vibhāvayantotata utthito kathayi dharma nirupalepam|taṃ cāpi nāmayi
jagārthanidāna bodhaunāsti triloka śubha tena samaṃ bhaveyā||5||

taṃ caiva puṇya puna khyāyati riktamevatatha śūnya tuccha vaśikaṃ ca asārakaṃ ca|evaṃ carantu
caratī sugatāna prajñāṃcaramāṇu puṇyu parigṛhṇati aprameyam||6||

abhilāpamātra ima jānati sarvadharmāṃbuddhena deśita prayukta prakāśitāṃśca|kalpāna koṭinayutāṃ
bahu bhāṣamāṇona ca kṣīyate na ca vivardhati dharmadhātuḥ||7||

ye cāpi pañca imi pāramitā jinānā-mete'pi dharma parikīrtita nāmamātrāḥ|pariṇāmayāti na ca manyati
bodhisattvona ca hīyate spṛśati uttamabuddhabodhim||8||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ
śūnyatāparivarto nāmāṣṭādaśamaḥ
