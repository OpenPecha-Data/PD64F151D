13

yo eva paśyati sa paśyati sarvadharmānsarvānamātyakiriyā ti upekṣya rājā|yāvanti buddhakriya
dharmata śrāvakāṇāṃprajñāya pāramita sarva karoti tāni||1||

na ca rāja grāma vrajate na ca rājyarāṣṭrānsarvaṃ ca ādadati so viṣayātu āyam|na ca bodhisattva
calate kvaci dharmatāyāṃsarvāṃśca ādadati ye guṇa buddhadharme||2||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāmacintyaparivarto
nāma trayodaśamaḥ||
