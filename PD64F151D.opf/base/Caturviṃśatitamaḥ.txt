24

māro'pi tasmi samaye bhavate saśalyośokātu duḥkhitu anantamano'lpasthāmo|diśadāha ulka kṣipate
bhayadarśanārthaṃkathameṣa dīnamanaso bhavi bodhisattvo||1||

[yatha te bhavanti vidu āśayasaṃprayuktādivarātri prajñavarapāramitārthadarśī|]tada kāyacitta
khagapakṣisatulyabhūtāavatāru so kutu labhiṣyati kṛṣṇabandhuḥ||2||

kalahāvivādupagatā yada bodhisattvābhonti parasparaviruddhaka ruṣṭacittāḥ|tada māra tuṣṭu bhavatī
paramaṃ udagroubhi eti dūra bhaviṣyanti jināna jñāne||3||

ubhi eti dūri bhaviṣyanti piśācatulyāubhi eti ātma kariṣyanti pratijñahānim|duṣṭāna kṣāntivikalāna
kuto'sti bodhitada māru tuṣṭu bhavatī namucīsapakṣo||4||

yo bodhisattva ayu vyākṛtu vyākṛtasmiṃcittaṃ pradūṣayi vivādu samārabheyyā|yāvanti cittakṣaṇikā
khiladoṣayuktā-stāvanta kalpa puna saṃnahitavya bhonti||5||

atha tasyupadyati matīti aśobhanā tikṣāntīya pāramita bodhi spṛśanti buddhāḥ|pratidarśayāti
puna āyati saṃvarāṇiapayāti vā sa iha śikṣati buddhadharme||6||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāmabhimānaparivarto
nāma caturviṃśatitamaḥ||
