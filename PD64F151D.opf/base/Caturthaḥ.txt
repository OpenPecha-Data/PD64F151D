4

śakro jinena paripṛcchitu praśnamāhusaci gaṅgavālikasamā siya buddhakṣetrāḥ|jinadhātu sarvi
paripūrita cūḍibaddhāimameva prajñavarapāramitāhu gṛhṇe||1||

kiṃ kāraṇaṃ na mi śarīri agauravatvaṃapi tū khu prajñaparibhāvita pūjayanti|yatha rājaniśrita
naro labhi sarvi pūjāṃtatha prajñapāramitaniśrita buddhadhātuḥ||2||

maṇiratna sarvi guṇayukta anarghaprāptoyasmiṃ karaṇḍaki bhave sa namasyanīyaḥ|tasyāpi uddhṛta
spṛhanti karaṇḍakasmiṃtasyaiva te guṇa mahāratanasya bhonti||3||

emeva prajñavarapāramitāguṇāniyannirvṛte'pi jinadhātu labhanti pūjām|tasmā hu tān jinaguṇā(n)
parighettukāmoso prajñapāramita gṛhṇatu eṣa mokṣo||4||

pūrvaṃgamā bhavatu dānu dadantu prajñāśīle ca kṣānti tatha vīrya tathaiva dhyāne|parigrāhikā
kuśaladharmaavipraṇāśeekā ca sā api nidarśayi sarvadharmān|| 5||

yatha jambudvīpi bahuvṛkṣasahasrakoṭīnānāprakāra vividhāśca anekarūpāḥ|na vi chāyanānatu bhaveta
viśeṣatāpianyatra chāyagatasaṃkhya prabhāṣamāṇā||6||

emeva pañca imi pāramitā jinānāṃprajñāya pāramita nāmatayā bhavanti|sarvajñatāya pariṇāmayamāṇa
sarveṣaḍapīha ekanayamarchati bodhināmā||7||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ guṇaparikīrtanaparivarto
nāma caturthaḥ||
