9

evaṃ carantu vidu paṇḍitu bodhisattvosaṅgā uchinnu vrajate jagatī asakto|sūryo va rāhugrahamukta
virocamānoagnīva yukta tṛṇakāṣṭhavanaṃ dahāti||1||

prakṛtīya śuddha pariśuddhimi sarvadharmāṃprajñāya pāramita paśyati bodhisattvo |na ca paśyakaṃ
labhati nāpi ca sarvadharmāneṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā||2||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ
stutiparivarto nāma navamaḥ||
