16

ākāśadhātu purimādiśi dakṣiṇāyāṃtatha paścimottaradiśāya anantapārā|uparādharāya daśaddiśi yāvadastinānātvatā
na bhavate na viśeṣaprāptā||1||

atikrānta yā tathata yā tathatā aprāptāpratyutpanna yā tathata yā tathatārhatānām|yā sarvadharmatathatā
tathatārhatānāṃsarveṣa dharmatathatā na viśeṣaprāptā||2||

yo bodhisattva imi icchati prāpuṇetuṃnānātvadharmavigatāṃ sugatāna bodhim|prajñāya pāramita
yujyatu yāya yuktovina prajña nāstyadhigamo naranāyakānām||3||

pakṣisya yojanaśataṃ mahatātmabhāvopañcāśatā pi abalobhayakṣīṇapakṣo|so trāyatriṃśabhavanādiṣu
jambudvīpeātmānamosariyi taṃ vilayaṃ vrajeyyā||4||

yadyāpi pañca ima pāramitā jinānāṃbahukalpakoṭiniyutāṃ samudānayeyyā|praṇidhīnanantavipulāṃ
sada sevya lokeanupāya prajñavikalā pari śrāvakatve||5||

niryāyanāya ya icchati buddhajñānesamacitta sarvajagatī pitṛmātṛsaṃjñā|hitacitta maitramana
eva parākrameyyāakhilārjavo mṛdugirāya parākrameyyā||6||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ
tathatāparivarto nāma ṣoḍaśamaḥ||
