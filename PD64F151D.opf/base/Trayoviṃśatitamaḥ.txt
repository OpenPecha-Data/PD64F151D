23

udayāti sūryu vigatāśca marīcimālāvidhamitva sarva tamasākulamandhakāram|avibhonti sarva krimijotikaprāṇabhūtāṃsarvāṃśca
tārakagaṇānapi candraābhām||1||

emeva prajñavarapāramitāṃ carantovidhamitva dṛṣṭigahanaṃ vidu bodhisattvo |abhibhonti sarvajagatī
rahapratyayāṃścaśūnyānimittacarito pṛthu bodhisattvo||2||

yatha rājaputra dhanadāyaku arthakāmosarveṣu śreṣṭha bhavate abhigāminīye|sa hyeṣa etarahi sattva
pramocayātiprāgeva rājyasthitu bheṣyati paṭṭadhārī||3||

emeva prajñacarito vidu bodhisattvoamṛtasya dāyaku priyo marumānuṣāṇām|ayu eṣa eṣati hi sattvasukhābhiyuktoprāgeva
yāva sthitu bheṣyati dharmarājo||4||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ
śakraparivarto nāma trayoviṃśatitamaḥ||
