saci rūpa saṃjña api vedana cetanāyāṃcittaṃ anitya pariṇāmayi bodhisattvo|pra(ti)varṇikāya carate
aprajānamānona hi dharma paṇḍita vināśa karoti jātu||1||

yasmin na rūpa api vedana cāpi saṃjñāvijñāna naiva na pi cetanayopalabdhiḥ|anupādu śūnya na
ya jānati sarvadharmāneṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā||2||

yāvanti gaṅganadivālikatulyakṣetretāvanti sattva arahanti vineya kaścit|yaścaiva prajña ima
pāramitā likhitvāparasattvi pustaku dadeya viśiṣṭapuṇyaḥ||3||

kiṃ kāraṇaṃ ta iha śikṣita vādiśreṣṭhāgamayanti dharma nikhilāniha śūnyatāyām|yāṃ śrutva śrāvaka
spṛśanti vimukti śīghraṃpratyekabodhi spṛśayanti ca buddhabodhim||4||

asato'ṅkurasya drumasaṃbhavu nāsti lokekuta śākhapatraphalapuṣpaupādu tatra|vina bodhicitta
jinasaṃbhavu nāsti lokekuta śakrabrahmaphala śrāvakaprādubhāvaḥ||5||

ādityamaṇḍalu yadā prabhajāla muñcīkarmakriyāsu tada sattva parākramanti|tatha bodhicitta sada
lokavidusya jñātojñānena sarvaguṇadharma samāgamanti||6||

yatha nopatapta asato bhujagādhipasyakuta nadyaprasravu bhavediha jambudvīpe|asatā nadīya phalapuṣpa
na saṃbhaveyuḥna ca sāgarāṇa ratanā bhavi naikarūpāḥ||7||

tatha bodhicitta asatīha tathāgatasyakuta jñānaprasravu bhavediha sarvaloke|jñānasya co asati
nāsti guṇāna vṛddhiḥna ca bodhi sāgarasamā na ca buddhadharmāḥ||8||

yāvanti loki kvaci jotikaprāṇabhūtāobhāsanārtha prabha osarayanti sarve|varasūryamaṇḍalaviniḥsṛta
ekaraśmīna kalā pi jyotikagaṇe siya sarvaābhāḥ||9||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ
puṇyaparyāyaparivarto nāma pañcamaḥ||
