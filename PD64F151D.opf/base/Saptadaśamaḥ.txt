17

sthaviro subhūti paripṛcchati lokanāthaṃaraṇāya liṅga bhaṇahī guṇasāgarāṇām|avivartiyā yatha
bhavanti mahānubhāvātāṃ vyākuruṣva jinaguṇāna pradeśamātram||1||

nānātvasaṃjñavigatā gira yuktabhāṇīna ca anya te śramaṇa brāhmaṇa āśrayanti|triyapāyavarjita
vidū sadakāli bhontidaśabhiśca te kuśalakarmapathebhi yuktā||2||

dharmaṃ nirāmiṣu jagasyanuśāsayantiekāntadharmaniyatāḥ sada snigdhavākyāḥ|sthiticaṃkramaṃ śayaniṣadya
susaṃprajānāyugamātraprekṣiṇa vrajantyabhrāntacintā||3||

śuciśaucaambaradharā trivivekaśuddhāna ca lābhakāma vṛṣabhā sada dharmakāmāḥ|mārasyatītaviṣayā
aparapraṇeyācatudhyānadhyāyi na ca niśrita tatra dhyāne||4||

na ca kīrtikāma na ca krodhaparītacittāgṛhibhūta nitya anadhyoṣita sarva vastuṃ|na ca jīvikāviṣayabhoga
gaveṣayantiabhicāramantra na ca istriprayogamantrāḥ||5||

na ca ādiśanti puruṣaiḥ striya icchakarmāṃpravivikta prajñavarapāramitābhiyuktāḥ|kalahāvivādavigatā
dṛḍhamaitracittāsarvajñakāma sada śāsani nimnacittāḥ||6||

pratyantamlecchajanavarjitaantadeśāḥsvakabhūmi kāṅkṣavigatāḥ sada merukalpāḥ|dharmārtha jīvita
tyajanti prayuktayogāavivartiyāna imi liṅga prajānitavyā||7||bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāmavinivartanīyaliṅgākāraparivarto
nāma saptadaśamaḥ||
